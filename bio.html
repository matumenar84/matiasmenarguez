The Career.

My professional path began at the **Escuela de Música de Buenos Aires (EMBA)**, where I was awarded a scholarship, led to a degree as a Professional Musician and set the stage for two decades of performance and study.

The Musical Theater Years

A significant chapter of my career was defined by the technical and emotional demands of musical theater. I spent over a year performing weekly at **Teatro Nacional Cervantes**, one of Argentina’s most iconic cultural landmarks, for the production of *Cyrano de más acá*.

My work in theater extended to acclaimed, multiple awards-winning production *Los Monstruos*, experience that required a high degree of musical discipline, versatility, and the ability to support a narrative through rhythm.

Touring & International Performance

Beyond the theater, I have maintained a continuous presence in the indie and world music scenes. As a member of **Olvide San Telmo**, I toured extensively through Chile and the Argentinian Patagonia, performing live across the diverse music circuit of Buenos Aires.

My focus on jazz and improvised music led to performances at the **Buenos Aires International Jazz Festival** and the **Belize International Jazz Festival** with the *Perceptual Trio*. In New York City, I performed with my modern jazz duo, **The SoHo Rentals**, while continuing my education through private study with masters like **Mark Guiliana**, **Greg Hutchinson**, and **Daniel "Pipi" Piazzolla**.

Studio & Production

Today, I operate a home recording studio in Mount Olive, NJ. Over the last ten years, I have tracked hundreds of drums for international artists, composed for video games, and produced mixes for independent records. Whether recording or mixing a modern production, my approach is shaped by over 21 years of stage, studio, and touring experience.

Currently taking session and student inquiries from my NJ studio.
