The Studio Specs

The Hardware

  • Kits: Gretsch Round Badge 60s, Gretsch Jazz kit, vintage and modern snare drums and cymbals
  • Preamps: 2x Kultrumm TAB V376, 4x Behringer v1273, Audient ASP880
  • Audio Interface: Universal Audio Apollo x8p

The Software

  • DAW: LUNA

  • Delivery time: 24 to 48 hours
